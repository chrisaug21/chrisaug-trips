Day 8: Bruges

Explore the enchanting medieval city known as the "Venice of the North".

  • Evening stroll through the illuminated city center

Tips for Day 8

  • Book accommodation in advance as Bruges is very popular with tourists
  • The Bruges City Card offers free entry to most museums and attractions
  • Visit the Belfry early to avoid long queues

Day 8: Utrecht

Explore Utrecht's canals and historic center.

Morning

  • Arrival in Brussels International Airport
  • Navigate to train station at airport
  • Purchase train ticket to Ghent Sint-Pieters
  • Train journey (approximately 1 hour)

Afternoon

  • Check-in at accommodation in Ghent
  • Quick lunch at a local caf√©
  • Initial orientation walk around central Ghent
  • Visit Korenmarkt and Sint-Michielsbrug (St. Michael's Bridge) for iconic views

Evening

  • Dinner at a traditional Flemish restaurant
  • Stroll along the illuminated medieval buildings and canals
  • Early night to recover from travel fatigue

Tips for Day 2

  • Brussels Airport has clear signage to the train station below the terminal
  • Consider purchasing a travel card if you plan to use public transport in Ghent
  • Most accommodations in Ghent's center are within walking distance of main attractions
  • Ask your accommodation for a local map and recommendations

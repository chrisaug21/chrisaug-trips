Day 2: Amsterdam City Exploration

Discover the historic center and iconic attractions of Amsterdam.

Day 1 Day 2 Day 3 Day 4 Day 5 Day 6 Day 7 Day 8 Day 9 Day 10

Morning

  • Breakfast at your hotel or a local bakery
  • Visit the Anne Frank House (pre-booking essential)
  • Explore the Jordaan neighborhood

Afternoon

  • Lunch at a traditional Dutch café
  • Visit the Rijksmuseum (allow 2-3 hours)
  • Take photos at the I Amsterdam sign

Evening

  • Dinner in the De Pijp neighborhood
  • Optional: Red Light District walking tour
  • Enjoy a drink at a brown café (traditional Dutch pub)

Tips for Day 2

  • Book Anne Frank House tickets well in advance
  • The Rijksmuseum is large - plan which sections interest you most
  • Consider renting a bike for a more authentic Amsterdam experience

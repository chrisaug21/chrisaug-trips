Day 1: Travel Day

Begin your journey to Belgium & the Netherlands.

Morning

  • Final packing and preparation
  • Travel to departure airport
  • Check-in and security procedures

Afternoon

  • Board flight to Europe
  • In-flight meals and entertainment
  • Try to get some rest during the flight

Evening

  • Arrive in Europe (depending on flight time)
  • Possible connecting flight or overnight travel

Tips for Travel Day

  • Wear comfortable clothes for the long journey
  • Pack essentials in your carry-on (medications, change of clothes)
  • Download offline maps and entertainment for the journey
  • Stay hydrated during the flight
  • Set your watch to destination time to begin adjusting to the time difference

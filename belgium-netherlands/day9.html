Day 9: Antwerp

Discover Belgium's diamond capital and fashion hub.

Day 1 Day 2 Day 3 Day 4 Day 5 Day 6 Day 7 Day 8 Day 9 Day 10

Morning

  • Check out of Bruges accommodation
  • Train from Bruges to Antwerp (approx. 1.5 hours)
  • Check into Antwerp accommodation
  • Visit Antwerp Central Station (one of the world's most beautiful train stations)

Afternoon

  • Lunch in the historic center
  • Visit the Cathedral of Our Lady
  • Explore the Grote Markt (Great Market Square)
  • Tour the Rubens House (Rubenshuis), former home of painter Peter Paul Rubens

Evening

  • Stroll through the Fashion District
  • Explore MAS Museum (Museum aan de Stroom) with rooftop views
  • Dinner at a local restaurant
  • Visit a local bar to try Antwerp's De Koninck beer

Tips for Day 9

  • The Antwerp City Card offers free admission to museums and public transport
  • The Diamond District is closed on weekends
  • MAS Museum is free to visit the rooftop panorama, even if you don't enter the museum exhibits

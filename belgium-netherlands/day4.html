Day 4: Day Trip to Rotterdam

Experience the modern architecture and vibrant atmosphere of Rotterdam.

Day 1 Day 2 Day 3 Day 4 Day 5 Day 6 Day 7 Day 8 Day 9 Day 10

Morning

  • Early train from Amsterdam Centraal to Rotterdam (approx. 1 hour)
  • Visit the iconic Cube Houses (Kijk-Kubus)
  • Explore the Markthal (market hall with stunning ceiling artwork)

Afternoon

  • Lunch at one of the stalls in the Markthal
  • Visit the Rotterdam Centraal Station (architectural marvel)
  • Take the water taxi to Hotel New York and the SS Rotterdam

Evening

  • Dinner at a restaurant in the Witte de Withstraat area
  • Visit the Euromast observation tower for sunset views (optional)
  • Return to Amsterdam by train

Tips for Day 4

  • Buy a round-trip train ticket to save money
  • Consider purchasing the Rotterdam Welcome Card for discounts
  • Wear comfortable shoes as Rotterdam requires a lot of walking

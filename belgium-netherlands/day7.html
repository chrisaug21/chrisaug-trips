Day 7: Day Trip to Ghent

Experience one of Belgium's most beautiful medieval cities.

Day 1 Day 2 Day 3 Day 4 Day 5 Day 6 Day 7 Day 8 Day 9 Day 10

Morning

  • Early train from Brussels to Ghent (approx. 30 minutes)
  • Visit St. Bavo's Cathedral to see the famous Ghent Altarpiece
  • Explore the Belfry of Ghent for panoramic views

Afternoon

  • Lunch at one of the caf√©s at Korenmarkt or Graslei
  • Visit Gravensteen Castle (the Castle of the Counts)
  • Take a boat tour along the canals
  • Browse the Design Museum Gent or STAM (Ghent City Museum)

Evening

  • Dinner at one of Ghent's excellent restaurants
  • See Ghent's Light Plan illuminations after dark
  • Return to Brussels by train

Tips for Day 7

  • The CityCard Gent gives access to all museums and public transport
  • Thursdays are late-night shopping in Ghent
  • Consider taking the tram from Gent-Sint-Pieters station to the city center

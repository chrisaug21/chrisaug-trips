Day 6: Brussels City Center

Discover the historic heart of Brussels and sample Belgian specialties.

Day 1 Day 2 Day 3 Day 4 Day 5 Day 6 Day 7 Day 8 Day 9 Day 10

Morning

  • Breakfast at a local café with Belgian waffles
  • Explore Grand Place (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • Visit Manneken Pis and its lesser-known female counterpart, Jeanneke Pis
  • Browse the Brussels City Museum

Afternoon

  • Lunch with traditional Belgian food (mussels or carbonnade flamande)
  • Visit the Royal Palace of Brussels (open to public in summer)
  • Explore the Mont des Arts garden and surrounding museums
  • Visit the Musical Instruments Museum

Evening

  • Sample Belgian beers at Delirium Café (home to thousands of beer varieties)
  • Dinner in the Sainte-Catherine neighborhood
  • Evening walk through the Galeries Royales Saint-Hubert (elegant shopping arcade)

Tips for Day 6

  • Get a Brussels Card for free access to museums and public transport
  • Don't miss trying Belgian chocolate at the shops around Grand Place
  • Many museums close on Mondays - check opening times in advance
